# Autonomous SEO Plan

## Goal

Improve `kimward.co.uk` as a lead-generation page for Kim's UK Laravel/PHP contractor outreach.

Primary audience:

- UK agencies and small teams with inherited Laravel/PHP apps.
- Owner-led studios needing async overflow/rescue help.
- Teams with admin/reporting/internal-tool backlogs.
- Teams considering reviewed AI workflows for internal work.

Primary commercial intent:

- Get the recipient to email one bounded Laravel/admin/AI-workflow task to `hello@kimward.co.uk`.

Main offer:

## Operating cadence

### Nightly SEO Improvement Run

Runs during a low-traffic window.

Purpose: make one small, safe improvement and verify production.

### Weekly SEO Strategy Review

Runs Monday morning.

Purpose: compare crawl/Search Console/analytics trends where available, reprioritise `PLAN.md` and `BACKLOG.md`, and summarise work in `RUN_LOG.md`.

### Monthly Technical SEO Audit

Runs on the first day of each month.

Purpose: refresh `BASELINE.md`, recheck technical SEO fundamentals, and create/update backlog items.

## Near-term sequence

1. Add `/sitemap.xml` and include it in `robots.txt`.

## Acceptance rules

Every production SEO change must have:

- Local build/test pass unless blocked.
- Production HTTP verification.
- `RUN_LOG.md` entry with commit hash and checks.
- Rollback if production verification fails.

## Do not do

- Do not invent testimonials, client logos, private Tutorful metrics, or private internal system details.
- Do not pivot into WordPress/fixed-price/general agency positioning.
- Do not add noisy content pages purely for keyword stuffing.
- Do not ask Kim to choose routine SEO tasks.
